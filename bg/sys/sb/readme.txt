-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Installation:

First, create a new folder called "sb" in bg\sys and do in the sprites
and the dat.-file. 
Add the line  

       "id: 18 file: bg\sys\sb\bg.dat"  

at the background-part in the data.txt or change the sended data.txt 
with the original one which is in the game.
